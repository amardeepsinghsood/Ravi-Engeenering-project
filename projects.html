High-Purity API Solvent Recovery System

The Challenge

A leading European pharma manufacturer required a GMP-compliant system to recover multi-component mixed solvents from their API synthesis line to >99.8% purity, while minimising thermal degradation of heat-sensitive residuals.

The Solution

We engineered a fully automated, 30-stage continuous fractional distillation column operating under high vacuum. The system featured structured packing for low pressure drop and a PLC/SCADA control system for precise reflux management.

Essential OilsSteam Distillation

Turnkey Aromatherapy Oil Distillation Complex

The Challenge

A specialized aromatics producer in Southeast Asia required a massive scale-up of their steam distillation capabilities for tough fibrous materials, needing a system that could handle high steam pressures safely.

The Solution

Designed and installed a multi-vessel stainless steel steam distillation battery with integrated automated material handling (hydraulic lifting lids). Included highly-efficient multi-tube condensers and specialized oil-water separators tailored for varying specific gravities.

ChemicalsVacuum Evaporation

Corrosive Acid Concentration Plant

The Challenge

A specialty chemical manufacturer required an evaporation system to concentrate highly corrosive dilute acids, demanding extreme material compatibility and rigorous safety standards for ATEX zones.

The Solution

Built a custom multi-effect vacuum evaporation unit utilizing advanced exotic alloys and PTFE linings. The system achieved the required concentration levels while operating safely within the hazardous zone footprint.
